Layer 2 Supply Managers — 5 Category Groups BLR Bangalore · commercial ownership by supply type globally ▾

Layer 3 In-Destination — Product Managers & DMC Partners 50 destinations · 6 regions · contract all supply on the ground ▾
How this works: Each of our 50 destinations has one Product Manager or DMC partner who contracts all supply elements locally — hotels, transport, tours, restaurants, visa agents and all other types. They report functionally to the relevant Supply Manager in BLR by category (e.g. on hotel matters they speak to the Accommodation Manager; on visa matters to the Documentation Manager). As booking volume grows, each destination follows the growth path: individual → small team → in-destination office → potential DMC acquisition.
Supply Types 17 Supply Components — 5 Category Groups Contracted in-destination · loaded and QC'd in BLR · auto-creates products ▾
Why 17 types matter: Every TripFactory product package is assembled from one or more of these 17 supply components. When a supply item is contracted, loaded, QC-checked and tagged with its type, the platform assembles packages automatically. The more supply types covered in each destination, the richer and more complete the products the system can build.
Workflow From Contract to Live Product — 5 Steps Click each step to see what happens ▾
The key principle: The person who contracts supply never loads it. This two-step separation — contracting in-destination, loading in BLR — is the quality gate that catches rate errors, missing content and compliance gaps before anything goes live. Every step has a hard SLA, not a target.

Growth Model Destination Growth Path Volume-triggered · built into the model from day one · click each stage ▾
Built to scale: Every destination starts with a single PM or DMC partner and grows through defined stages triggered by booking volume. This means the cost structure scales with revenue — we never over-invest ahead of demand. At the top of the path, a DMC acquisition gives us full control of the supply chain in that destination.
